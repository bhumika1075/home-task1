Modi 3.0 Cabinet first meet today, likely to approve more rural houses: Sources

Posted on 19 June 2024.

The newly formed Modi 3.0 Cabinet will hold its first meeting at the Prime Minister's residence at 7, Lok Kalyan Marg. It is likely to approve two crore additional houses under the Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) in the meeting scheduled at 5 pm, according to government sources.Furthermore, the Union Cabinet is likely to increase the assistance provided to the beneficiaries under the PMAY-G by nearly 50 per cent.
